From Start To Knit

Knitting progress tracker

Following my college graduation, I was looking to take up a new hobby. Something both practical and creative. I began my knitting journey starting with dish cloths and scarves and slowing progressing to sweaters and lace. I created From Start to Knit to keep track of my knitting progress and document my projects. I hope to encourage others to record their hobbies and share what they've learned.
